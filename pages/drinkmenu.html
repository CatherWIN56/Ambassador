SEASONAL COCKTAILS
THE AMBO BLOODY MARY
The Ambassador’s own Bloody Mary, crafted with a six-pepper blend with just the right amount of heat, garnished with a snack stick, green olives & a pickle. (Also available in a Fishbowl size!)
APPLE CIDER MARGARITA
Cider season is here, and we are celebrating with this scrumptious variation of America’s favorite cocktail! Tequila, lemon, cinnamon simple syrup, and a touch of brandy make a Margarita that is perfect for watching the leaves change.
BLACK CAT SMASH
Blackberries muddled with rosemary, lemon, orange bitters, and tequila, topped with soda water. This eerie yet warming drink is perfect for those spooky nights ahead.
ESPRESSO MARTINI
Vodka, coffee liqueur, and cold brew concentrate, shaken and served in a martini glass. This caffeinated cocktail truly is the perfect post-dinner pick-me-up.
MAPLE BOURBON OLD FASHIONED
A Northern take on a whiskey favorite, crafted from bourbon barrel-aged maple syrup, Michigan’s Iron Fish Distillery bourbon, and a dash of Angostura bitters.
NORTHWEST PASSAGE
A beer cocktail made with 51k IPA, house made honey syrup, lemon juice, and Linie Aquavit: a spiced Scandinavian spirit that has been aged at sea for more than 2 years. This cocktail celebrates those with an adventurous spirit … here’s to you!
ROSEMARY PEAR MULE
Pear vodka, housemade rosemary simple syrup, lime juice, bitters, and ginger beer come together to create a sweet and slightly spiced variation on a modern classic.
SPICED GIN PUNCH
Gin and housemade autumn simple syrup are combined with pineapple juice, orange juice, tonic water, and clove bitters to create a delicious fall punch full of your favorite fall flavors!
